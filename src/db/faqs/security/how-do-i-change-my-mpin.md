## How do I change my MPIN?

1.  Login to the Eplayment app.
2.  Press the Settings button on the lower tab of your screen.
3.  Press Security.
4.  Enter your MPIN.
5.  Press Change MPIN.
6.  Enter your new MPIN and confirm.
